This matrix maps current route coverage to content depth and conversion utility so publishing can be prioritized by impact.

| Cluster | Existing Coverage | Gap | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| Core service pages | Strong (`/skilled-migration`, `/student-visa-australia`, `/partner-visa-australia`, `/employer-sponsored-visas`, `/visitor-visas`) | Few evergreen decision guides | Add practical planning guides and link from updates/service pages |

| Destination hubs | Broad coverage on `/destinations/[slug]` | Uneven depth for NZ/Canada/UK sections | Expand non-Australia section copy, FAQs, and related links |
| Trust / legal | Present (`/privacy-policy`, `/terms-of-use`, `/disclaimer`, `/code-of-conduct`) | Privacy/terms needed production wording and stronger trust visibility | Upgrade legal copy and improve trust profile content on About |
